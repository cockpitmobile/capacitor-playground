Reminders

Privacy Settings
Control what you share with the community.
Selfie sharing
Your selfies can be shared with the Run Across America community including featured teams, limited to your private teams, or kept private to you.
Activity sharing
Your activities can be shared with featured teams, limited to your private teams, or kept private to you.
Achievement sharing
Your achievements can be shared with featured teams, limited to your private teams, or kept private to you.
Notification Settings
Notifications keep you updated on the season and on social activity in your teams. To customize what you receive for individual teams look for the icon on your team's page.
Season notifications
Reminders when it's time to start, and other important announcements.
Comment notifications
Notifications for comments on your activities and comments in threads you are apart of.
Team Settings
Control how you interact with teams.
Team goal expired alert
Alert to notify you when your private team goal has expired.
Email Settings
Email notifications keep you updated on your season progress and activities. Select what you'd like us to notify you about.
